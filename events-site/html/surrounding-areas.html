Within walking distance of the university is downtown Davis, home to a collection of shops, art galleries, eateries and a popular farmers’ market. The attractions of downtown Davis reflect its small town yet progressive personality, and presents guests with an array of options.

Located in the heart of northern California, UC Davis is only fifteen minutes from downtown Sacramento and a short drive from world renowned vacation destinations San Francisco, Napa Valley, and Lake Tahoe. Simply put, Davis’ central location allows guests to spend the day touring a metropolitan city, wine tasting, or hitting the slopes of the Sierras.
