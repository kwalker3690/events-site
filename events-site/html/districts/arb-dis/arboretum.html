Lush and peaceful, the Arboretum is a beautiful location for an array of events. This 100-acre public garden is situated nearby the University Gateway and is home to over 4,000 types of trees and plants. With eighteen distinct collections, the Arboretum is sure to be the perfect backdrop for any type of event.
