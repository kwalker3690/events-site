Known for its eye catching architecture and acoustic excellence, Mondavi Center’s 1,800-seat Jackson hall is gaining an international reputation as a superb performance space. Its sound and lighting systems can be fine-tuned — acoustically and otherwise — for all types of events from concerts to Broadway productions, to lectures and commencements.

The 3,000 square foot Studio Theater features a window wall overlooking an outdoor plaza and the UC Davis Arboretum. This fully equipped theater sets the stage for dance, drama, and music performances, and can also be set up as a dining or lecture space.

This oval-shaped room located on the second level of the theater lobby has views to the plaza and gardens below. Its intimate setting is designed for conversation and light refreshments among friends, colleagues, family or dignitaries.
