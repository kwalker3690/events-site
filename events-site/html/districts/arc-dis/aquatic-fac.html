Aquatic facilities to accommodate both recreational and competitive events are available when you plan an athletic event at UC Davis. Surrounded by a grassy lawn fit for pool side lounging, the Recreation Pool is perfect for barbeques and personal events.

Hickey Pool's 25-yard lanes are available and heated year-round for lap swimming. Conveniently located within the Hickey Gym facility, the gym's locker rooms and facilities are accessible from the pool.

Schaal Aquatic Center provides northern California with a setting for competitive swimming, diving and aquatic sports.
