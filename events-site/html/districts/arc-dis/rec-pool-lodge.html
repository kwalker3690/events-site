With its natural wood-paneled interior, floor-to-ceiling windows, a stone fireplace and balcony, the Recreation Pool Lodge lends itself to a variety of events from business meetings to social functions.

The Meeting Room has hardwood flooring and a gas fireplace. The smaller West Lounge is well suited for lectures and breakout sessions and can also serve as a buffet area. A spacious tree-lined patio has two built-in barbeque grills and can be set up for outdoor dining. The grounds include an expansive lawn area and large recreational swimming pool.
