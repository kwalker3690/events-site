Located in the Quad District, this multi-faceted auditorium provides an excellent setting for banquets, auctions, weddings, conferences, concerts and tradeshows. In addition to a spacious main floor area, Freeborn Hall features a proscenium stage, theatrical lighting, two backstage dressing rooms and shows, and an on-site box office. Lighting, sound and audiovisual equipment can be used to enhance stage presentations, concerts, or event set-up.
