Equipment rentals to meet your event’s needs are available through UC Davis. Centerpieces, lighting accents and floral décor are just a few of the rental options available at a low cost.

UC Davis Special Services provides competitively priced rental, delivery, and set0up for tables, chairs, tents and canopies. Special Services prides itself on exceeding its customers’ expectations and works hard to make your experience enjoyable.

Campus Unions Event Rentals at UC Davis was developed specifically for the growing needs of the campus community. Their mission is to provide simple, yet effective decorations and displays at a reasonable cost. They offer a broad range of items (e.g. tables, chairs, lighting, centerpieces) to enhance conferences, banquets, luncheons, retirement parties, and many other get-togethers.
