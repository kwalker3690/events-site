When it comes to affordable comfort, look no further than on-campus lodging. UC Davis offers traditional dormitory style housing as well as a recently opened Hyatt Place Hotel in the University Gateway District.

Hyatt Place

173 Old Davis Rd, Extension
Davis, CA 95616
Phone: (530) 756-9500
ucdavis.place.hyatt.com

Set in the University Gateway, this contemporary hotel has an on-site pool and complimentary Wi-fi in every room. Hyatt Place offers easy access to I-80 and ample parking.

Conference Housing

confhsg@ucdavis.edu
Phone: (530) 752-8000
www.confhsg.ucdavis.edu

Coupled with all-inclusive meal plans, conference housing is the most affordable and convenient way for groups to experience UC Davis. Over 4,000 beds on campus host a variety of guests from cheer camp participants to conference attendees each summer.

Davis and the surrounding communities offer a variety of accommodations.
