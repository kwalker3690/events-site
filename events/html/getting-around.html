As a college town, Davis offers a variety of transportation opportunities.

Known as the nationâ€™s bike capitol, Davis offers hundreds of miles of bike paths. To explore UC Davis like the locals do, rent a bike from the UC Davis Bike Barn or visit one of the local bike shops downtown.

Get around town in one of our four vintage double-decker buses imported from London, or in one of our other 42 transit buses that make up to 600 trips per day. Visit UCD Unitrans for more information.

Davis is conveniently located at the intersection of Interstate 80, Highway 113 and ten minutes from Interstate 5 and Highway 99. Discover Davis with an area map or the University with a campus map.

Air travel to the Davis area is convenient and hassle free. Sacramento International Airport is only 25 minutes away and is serviced by 16 major national and international carriers. To arrange for an airport shuttle, visit the Davis Airporter.
