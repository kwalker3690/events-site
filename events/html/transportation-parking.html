Transportation and parking is hassle free at UC Davis. Whether you need to get to the airport, go wine tasting in Napa or reserve parking for 500 vehicles, UC Davis has you covered.

To ensure your event runs smoothly, let UC Davis Fleet Services Charter Bus design the best solution for your transportation needs. Fleet Services has the knowledge, experience and infrastructure to handle all your complex transportation needs from pre-event planning, airport transfers and event shuttles, to moving groups of 10 to 300 or more. Fleet Services is uniquely qualified to handle events that involve multiple origination and destination points. The Fleet Services team knows what it takes to make your event transportation run smoothly.

Parking is no problem with personal assistance from UC Davis transportation and Parking Services (TAPS). TAPS offers conference permits, reserved parking, bus parking, access to alternatives for loading/unloading, maps and parking information, as well as event parking attendants for all of your event needs.

Everything you need is already here! Call Conference and Event Services at (530)752-2675 or email events@ucdavis.edu
