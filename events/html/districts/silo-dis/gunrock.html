Gunrock Pub Café is a full service restaurant that offers a contemporary and eclectic menu based on locally grown, seasonal ingredients. A private landscaped patio compliments the space.

The café is easily accessible from many meeting rooms on campus and may also be reserved for private catered events. Next door, the Silo Union food court and Silo Bookstore are also open to the public.
