Schaal Aquatic Center provides northern California with a modern setting for competitive swimming, diving, and aquatic sports. This exceptional facility offers spectator seating and diving boards.

It also features a movable bulkhead, locker rooms and a versatile 65 meter x 25 yard pool which can be made to accommodate 8 to 24 lanes. Those planning aquatic competitions will find that Schaal has all of the essential amenities for aquatic events.
