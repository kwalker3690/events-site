The elegant Mondavi Center for the Arts welcomes guests to the campus as the district’s focal point. Across the street, the Alumni and Visitor Center and the UC Davis Conference Center frame the charming green space of the Vanderhoef Quad and offer unique spaces to accommodate formal and casual, as well as indoor and outdoor events and celebrations. Next to the university’s teaching vineyard is the cheerful Good Life Garden - an amiable host to events.
