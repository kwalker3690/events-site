The UC Davis Conference Center is a state-of-the-art learning and meeting complex adjacent to the Hyatt Place Hotel. A blend of comfort, versatility and style, the Conference Center includes a large multi-configuration ballroom, multiple meeting and conference rooms, a restaurant and pub, lobby and support space.

The adjacent three story hotel includes 75 guest rooms, interior hallways, lobby, and swimming pool.
