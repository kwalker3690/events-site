This ever-changing, edible landscape features organic and sustainably-grown vegetables, herbs and flowers. Essentially a living kitchen, the Good Life Garden includes interpretive signage about the cultivation and benefits of seasonal, local foods. Located in the courtyard of the Robert Mondavi Institute for Wine and Food Science in the University Gateway, the surrounding area is reminiscent of an old world villa, with the garden set amidst burnt orange and ochre stucco walls and flanked by a vineyard.

Amenities of the Good Life Garden include complimentary water service, ice machine and refrigerator, food staging area, and audiovisual equipment. A grand piano is also available.
