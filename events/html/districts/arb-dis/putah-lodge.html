Overlooking Putah Creek in the southwest section of the UC Davis Arboretum, Putah Creek Lodge has many indoor and outdoor amenities. Inside, the focal point is a distinctive and cozy fireplace. French doors lead to a patio which circles the building and overlooks acres of beautifully landscaped grounds.

Outdoor amenities include a large built-in barbeque, picnic tables, a well shaded picnic area and an amphitheater with bench seating around a fire pit which can be used for campfire presentations, lectures or ceremonies.
