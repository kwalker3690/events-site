Planning a conference or meeting? Look no further than Conference and Event Services at UC Davis. Our event planning team offers logistical planning, on-site coordination, and all the amenities of the University for events of all kinds. Our services include online registration and abstract submission, audio/visual rentals, catering, lodging arrangements and much more!

Our expert event coordinators will handle all of your registration needs. For both on and off-campus events our team can arrange for printed material, on-site registration, mailing and credit card processing. Conference and Event Services offers five registration and coordination packages as well as four on-line abstract submission packages.
